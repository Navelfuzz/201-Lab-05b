About Me

Where I've Been

Hello! My name is Jonathon Stillson, but I typically go by Jon. I'm originally from Grand Haven Michigan, but I've moved around all my life and lived in MI, MN, AK, NC, CA, NY, VA, FL, and MA.

What I've Learned

After high school early graduation in 2006, I joined the USCG and went to a year long "A School" to become an Electronics Technician. In 2017 I used TA through the USCG and credits given for military training programs to obtain an AAS in Electronics Engineering Technology. In 2022 I completed an AS in Computer Science at Tidewater Community College. In 2022/2023 I completed some courses in the BS Computer Science program at Old Dominion University.

What I've Done

I was an Electronics Technician in the USCG for 12 years. I joined in 2006 at the age of 17. Upon my separation in 2018 I was a First Class Petty Officer. My primary duties were to the maintenance and repair of electronic communications and navigation equipment.
